Discover coastal charm at Hotel Milo Santa Barbara, an oceanfront retreat just a leisurely 4-minute stroll from iconic Stearns Wharf. Immerse yourself in the vibrant hues of our modern rooms, each equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, iPod dock, and custom coffee machine. With two heated outdoor pools, a fitness center, and a hot tub, relaxation is at your fingertips. Enjoy a patio dining experience surrounded by a tropical garden or explore nearby attractions like the Santa Barbara Winery and Amtrak station, both within a 10-minute walk. Embrace the romance of our prime downtown location, rated 9.4 for couples.

More about this hotel

  • Hotel Milo Santa Barbara

    At Hotel Milo Santa Barbara, expect a charming seaside retreat with a blend of classic California style and modern amenities. Nestled near the waterfront, you'll enjoy breathtaking ocean views and easy access to Stearns Wharf and downtown attractions. The hotel exudes a laid-back, coastal ambiance with Spanish-inspired decor. Immerse yourself in comfortable rooms, a heated outdoor pool, and inviting courtyards. Attentive service, coastal cuisine at The Greenhouse and proximity to local gems create a memorable coastal escape at Hotel Milo Santa Barbara.

  • Who is this for?

    Guests at Hotel Milo Santa Barbara seek a blend of coastal charm and modern comfort. Captivated by its beachfront location, they appreciate the Mediterranean-inspired architecture and lush gardens. A mix of leisure and business travelers, they value the hotel's proximity to Stearns Wharf, State Street, and the Funk Zone. With a penchant for relaxed elegance, these guests enjoy personalized service, ocean views, and the laid-back sophistication that the hotel offers.

  • Why Choose Hotel Milo Santa Barbara?

    Hotel Milo Santa Barbara offers a captivating blend of coastal charm and modern comfort. Nestled along the iconic Californian coastline, this boutique hotel provides a serene escape with its lush gardens and scenic views. Indulge in stylish accommodations, personalized service, and proximity to Santa Barbara's attractions. With a relaxed ambiance and a commitment to guest satisfaction, Hotel Milo ensures a delightful stay, making it the perfect choice for a memorable coastal retreat.

  • Good to Know

    Hotel Milo Santa Barbara, located in California, offers a charming seaside retreat with a blend of modern comfort and coastal elegance. Nestled near Stearns Wharf and State Street, the hotel provides easy access to Santa Barbara's attractions. Guests can enjoy stylish accommodations, a heated outdoor pool, and Mediterranean-inspired gardens. The hotel's coastal-inspired design and personalized service create a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere for a memorable stay in this picturesque coastal town.

  • Reviews

Guests appreciate the comfortable rooms with ocean or garden views, the heated pool, and the inviting courtyard. The hotel's Spanish-style architecture adds to its appeal, creating a relaxing and picturesque atmosphere. Overall, visitors often commend Hotel Milo Santa Barbara for providing a delightful and enjoyable coastal retreat.
